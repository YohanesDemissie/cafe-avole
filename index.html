    • Cafe Avole

    Local Owned & Community Based

    Found by the Seattle Native, community builder and people lover, Solomon Malaku Doobie. Found in 2008, started as a market store but always had a greater vision to server his community. He now runs an ethnic Ethiopian coffee shop to serve other community builders, as well as hosting events to bring the community together. Cafe Avole has a cozy, welcoming environment with friendly regulars and warming atmosphere.
